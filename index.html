About Pranay

Hi, I'm Pranay Ranjan. I'm an event organiser and a community builder by passion and a Biotech Engineer by profession. I've completed my graduation from SRM, KTR Campus, Chennai. Like all other freshmen, I was perplexed and ended up joining various technical and non technical clubs to find out what I really wanted to do. CSI is the organising committee of Aaruush, SRM's techno management fest and working in CSI not only skilled me with event planning and organising but also enriched me with discipline and harmony. As a sophomore, I worked with few startups and developed my taste in corporate culture. My hardships paid me and I got promoted to Committee Member from volunteer. I decided to build a team of 50 diligent members and guided them to successfully conduct Aaruush'17. This is when Directorate of Alumni Affairs approached me with a proposal of working in the team. It took me just a single day to grow it from 5 to 35 members and 6 months to build a strong community of 70+ members.Working in DAA and conduction various events like Internship Fairs, Achiever's Connect, Alumni Day helped me develop my skills even more and there was no looking back since then in my 3 years tenure. I got promoted from Volunteer to Head Coordinator and from Joint Secretary to Vice-president. That's how I became a better version of myself.
